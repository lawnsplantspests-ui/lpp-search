PA Plants Importer
Turn a PA Plants search into rows for the Google Sheet
How to use it:
  1. In PA Plants, search a county and show the results. Select all the results (every page) and copy them.
  2. Paste into the box below and tap Convert.
  3. Tap Copy on the Apiary box → in the Sheet's Apiary tab, click the first empty cell in column A and paste.
  4. Tap Copy on the Hypersensitive box → do the same in the Hypersensitive tab.
  5. Open the Geocoder page and run it to fill in map locations for the new addresses.
🐝 Apiary rows
For the Apiary tab. Paste into the first empty row (column A), or download and open the file.
⚠️ Hypersensitive rows
For the Hypersensitive tab. Paste into the first empty row (column A), or download and open the file.
